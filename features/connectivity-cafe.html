About Connectivity Café

The Connectivity Café is a dynamic, open-access space within the JCU Ideas Lab designed to bring together students, founders, mentors, researchers, and industry partners in an informal and welcoming environment.

Whether you're looking to pitch your idea to someone new, catch up with a mentor over coffee, or simply find a space to work alongside like-minded innovators, the Connectivity Café is your go-to destination on campus.

Regular events, speaker sessions, and community meetups are hosted here — making it one of the most active spaces at JCU for cross-disciplinary collaboration. The café-style layout encourages spontaneous conversations that often spark the most interesting ideas.

As part of the JCU Ideas Lab's Connect pillar, the Connectivity Café helps bridge the gap between academia and industry — giving JCU community members a place to grow their networks, share knowledge, and find collaborators for projects big and small.
